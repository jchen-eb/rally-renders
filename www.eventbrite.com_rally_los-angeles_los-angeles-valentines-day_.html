How to Celebrate Your Love in Los Angeles this Valentine’s Day

By Matthew O'Shannessy / January 22, 2019
   
   

You could play it safe with dinner and a dozen roses this Valentine’s Day. Or, you could up your game and surprise the object of your affections with tickets to a secret masked ball. Why not gift your love some delicious heart-shaped macarons you created under the guidance of a renowned French chef while you’re at it? Then, to win their heart forever, throw in a romantic picnic with goats in the San Gabriel Mountains.

Remember: anything’s possible in Los Angeles — even a serious relationship.

Valentine’s Day Wreath Making Class

February 03, 1:00 PM — 3:00 PM

Topanga Nursery

Love is in the air! Join us in the Topanga Nursery for a special Valentine's Day Wreath-Making Class. During this two hour workshop, participants will learn how to make a live succulent wreath.

$63 / RSVP

Pre-Valentine’s Day Bash with The Dollface Dames

Feb 08, 10:30 PM — Feb 09, 12:30 AM

Magicopolis

Enjoy a show-stopping night of choreographed burlesque, aerial and variety acts, singing, and even "boy-lesque"! Also includes vendors, magic bartenders, and a red carpet entrance.

$28 - $107 / RSVP

Cupid’s Undie Run

February 09, 12:00 PM — 4:00 PM

The Buffalo Club

Take your pants off for charity this Valentine's Day and join in a "brief" fun run through Santa Monica to raise money for neurofibromatosis (NF)!

$40 / RSVP

Valentine’s Macaron Baking Class

February 09, 1:30 PM — 4:00 PM

APEX THE ONE

Executive Chef at Bijoux Macarons, Chef Vincent Guiheneuf, shows you how to bake heart shaped macarons with raspberry-chocolate ganache filling just in time for Valentine's Day!

$87 - $97 / RSVP

Secret Valentine’s Day Masked Ball

Feb 14, 8:00 PM — Feb 15, 1:00 AM

Secret Location, Downtown Los Angeles

Spend your Valentine's Day in a mysterious secret location in Downtown LA, complete with champagne, live jazz band, and burlesque shows. Black tie dress code. Location revealed two hours prior.

$134 / RSVP

Grand Park Lovers Rock

February 14, 6:00 PM — 11:00 PM

Grand Park

Groove to the soulful vibes of Lovers Rock reggae, bring your own special picnic dinner or enjoy the food trucks, and take a moonlit walk through the park amidst the iconic views of Los Angeles.

Free

February 14, 7:30 PM — 11:00 PM

Cinespia's annual Valentine's Day party is back with True Romance on the big screen and five floors of bars, DJs, and live bands!

By Matthew O'Shannessy

Matthew O’Shannessy is a writer living in Los Angeles. He grew up in Tasmania, Australia and moved to LA via Melbourne where he joined an art collective and worked for the Melbourne International Film Festival. His work has been published in various magazines and he’s currently co-editing an arts anthology. He lives in Echo Park, but you can often find him scouting out the San Fernando Valley.
